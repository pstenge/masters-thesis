\section{Research Objectives}

The main objective of this research is to develop a precise localisation system for autonomous vineyard robots that enables accurate spatial alignment between the robot, the 3D models, and the environment.

Sub-objectives include:

\begin{itemize}

    \item Quantify the localisation accuracy required for robotic pruning.

   \item Design scientific experiments and establish quantitative performance metrics to evaluate localisation accuracy. 
   
   \item Achieve the necessary localisation accuracy from sub-objective 1 in vineyard environments.
   
   \item Demonstrate the practical application of the developed system by enabling precise robotic manipulation tasks such as pruning within the vineyard environment.
   
   \item Validate localisation performance under varying vineyard conditions including GNSS dropout, lighting changes, and seasonal variations.
   
\end{itemize}
